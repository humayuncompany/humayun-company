Manufacturing Infrastructure

Our Complete Vertical Setup

By maintaining an independent vertical setup, we exercise complete oversight over the winding, dyeing, and drying processes, ensuring every cone meets rigorous industrial standards.

We operate imported **Taiwanese and Chinese cone winders** equipped with advanced length counters. This guarantees precise yarn package density, optimal cone formation, and uniform unwinding tension during high-speed sewing.

🧪 Advanced Cone Dyeing & Dryers

Our dye house utilizes **different sizes of high-pressure Cone Dyeing machines** tailored for flexible lot sizes, paired with specialized industrial cone dryers that eliminate inner-cone moisture and lock in color fastness.
